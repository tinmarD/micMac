******************************
       Modify a Signal
******************************

To access a signal information, modify its name, or delete the signal, go to the **Signals** menu and click on the name:

.. figure:: /_images/signals_modify_signal_menu.png
   :align: center
 
The following window appears:

.. figure:: /_images/signals_modify_signal_popup.png
   :align: center

This window sums up the signal's properties. The signal's name can be modify with the **Rename** button. The signal can also be deleted using the **Delete Signal** button.

.. note:: 
   Only raw signals can be renamed or deleted. When renaming a raw signal, all the child signals will also be renamed. Similarly, deleting a raw signal will also delete all child signals.
