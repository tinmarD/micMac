*****************
  Modify a view
*****************

The view position and its color can be modified. Views can also be deleted. 

The first way to modify a view is to click on its name on the **Views** menu : 

.. figure:: /_images/view_menu.png
   :align: center

The following window appears : 

.. figure:: /_images/view_popup.png
   :align: center

It is also possible to modify view's position or color or to delete the view by right-clicking on the correponding axis : 

.. figure:: /_images/view_right_click.png
   :align: center

.. note:: 
   Deleting a view will not delete the signal associated with this view.
